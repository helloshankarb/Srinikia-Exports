# **App Name**: SBNB Global Foods

## Core Features:

- Responsive Product Showcase: Display a comprehensive range of spices, pickles, vegetables, and masala powders with rich details, images, categorization, and integrated enquiry options, adaptable across all device sizes.
- Dynamic Informational Sections: Implement interactive and animated sections for 'About Us', 'Why Choose Us', 'Business Gallery', 'Client Testimonials', and 'Certifications' to convey company credibility and offerings effectively.
- Advanced Enquiry and Contact System: Centralized contact forms and product-specific enquiry modals with client-side validation, integrated with EmailJS for direct communication without needing a dedicated backend database.
- Full-Width Hero Auto Slider: An engaging, auto-advancing hero section with crossfade transitions, navigation controls, and clear calls-to-action to highlight diverse product categories and brand messaging.
- Global Export Statistics: Visually represent key business metrics (clients, countries exported, years of experience) with animated count-up numbers to build trust and demonstrate reach.
- Content and SEO Management Tools: Tools for generating SEO-friendly meta tags for individual pages and managing blog content to improve search engine visibility and inform customers about industry trends.
- AI-Assisted Marketing Copy Generation: A tool that generates compelling marketing-oriented text snippets for product descriptions or blog post outlines based on user input, ensuring fresh and engaging content.

## Style Guidelines:

- A rich and premium deep forest green (#1a4a2e) forms the brand's core, symbolizing natural purity and sophistication. It will be predominantly used for headings, primary calls-to-action, and key brand elements, establishing an earthy and luxurious aesthetic.
- A luxurious, premium gold (#c9a84c) serves as the secondary color, symbolizing prosperity and the exceptional quality of SBNB's products. It will highlight interactive elements, accents, and important textual information.
- A soft, premium off-white (#F0F5F2) provides a clean and elegant canvas for content sections, subtly reinforcing the natural theme with its gentle greenish undertone, ensuring readability and a sophisticated feel.
- Vibrant and rich accent colors — Turmeric Yellow (#e8a020) and Chili Red (#c0392b) — directly evoke the authentic, premium ingredients of Indian cuisine, adding warmth and visual interest to product cards and thematic elements.
- Cream White (#f9f5ef), as an accent, offers a sophisticated, clean contrast for textual elements or lighter backgrounds, maintaining the overall premium aesthetic.
- All text will use 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for its modern, precise, and highly legible appearance. It will be utilized for both headlines and body text, with specific weights (400, 600, 700) employed for hierarchical emphasis. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- The brand logo will feature a green leaf + spice SVG icon alongside the company name. Other icons throughout the site (e.g., cargo ship, magnifying glass, shield for 'What We Do'; social media icons; abstract symbols for stats like globe and calendar) will be modern, clean, and either line art or flat designs. Grayscale logos for memberships will turn full color on hover.
- The design will be fully mobile-responsive, adapting multi-column desktop grids (e.g., 4-column product grids, 2-column contact layouts) to tablet (2-column) and single-column mobile views. Key features include a sticky navigation header that changes on scroll, a full-screen slide-in hamburger menu for mobile, and content sections with intentional spacing for readability and visual appeal.
- Subtle and elegant animations will enhance the user experience. Sections will gracefully fade-in-up on scroll (using Framer Motion). Key statistics will feature animated count-up numbers. Carousels (hero, testimonials) will have smooth transitions and auto-advancing functionality. Interactive elements like product cards will lift slightly with icons changing color on hover. A branded page loader animation will be displayed on initial load.
